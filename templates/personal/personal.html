Personal

The cat is an artificial intelligence generated image of beloved 90s sitcom character Salem Saberhagen!

Salem Saberhagen is a 500-year-old witch who was sentenced to spend 100 years as a cat by the Witches Council as punishment for attempting to take over the world.

Salem is one of my favourite characters. Others include Gregory House from House M.D, Sherlock from Sherlock Holmes, and Marshall Eriksen from How I Met Your Mother.

In my free time, I really do music and theatre. I play guitar, and am in fact part of a charity concert in October 2022. I currently own a Fender Dreadnought Acoustic, as well as an Orange Stratocaster. I am hoping to soon get my hands on an Epiphone Es-339.

I also occasionally take part in local theatre productions. I played Jeff Fowlin in an instagram series called "Fowlin' Around with Jeff Fowling", a minor villain in "The Way Back" a Maltese film series, and shortly I will be playing Lancelot Gobbo in a local prodcution of the Merchant of Venice.

I have many great loves in my life, music and computing are two of them, and it is a big dream of mine to eventually combine these two into a singular pursuit. I often do music computing projects, and am especially a fan of music analytics.
