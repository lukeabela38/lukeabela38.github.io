Pose Estimation - An Introduction

Pose estimation in machine learning allows computers to understand and track human body movements in images or videos. It works by identifying key points on a person's body, like elbows, knees, and shoulders. In table tennis, this technology can be applied to analyze players' movements in real-time. Coaches can use it to assess a player's stance, footwork, and stroke techniques, identifying areas for improvement such as grip adjustments or refining swing techniques. Additionally, pose estimation could be integrated into training simulations or virtual reality environments, providing players with instant feedback on their movements and enabling more efficient practice sessions.
